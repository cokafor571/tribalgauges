Shipping Policy

Our goal is to offer you the best shipping options, no matter where you live. Every week, we deliver to hundreds of customers across the world, ensuring that we provide the very highest levels of responsiveness to you at all times.

The time frame for order delivery is divided into two parts:

Processing time: Order verification, tailoring, quality check and packaging. All orders are processed within 24-48 hours after the order is placed.

Shipping time: This refers to the time it takes for items to be shipped from our warehouse to the destination. International delivery usually takes about 7–14 business days. U.S orders are shipped by USPS which is a US Postal Service product. After processing and leaving the manufacturer, items usually take between 3-5 business days to arrive at their destination but can take longer on occasion.

Free Shipping is available on all U.S orders and a fixed rate of $7.99 for international orders outside the U.S.

Returns and Exchanges Policy

We handle returns on a case by case basis. We stand behind our goods and services and strive to offer the best product. Exchanges are valid for a 30 day period after the product is purchased.
